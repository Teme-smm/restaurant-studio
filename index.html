— The process

Four quiet weeks until you have a feed worth lingering on.

— Week 1

We talk.

A short message exchange. You tell me about the room, the menu, the mood. I do the listening.

— Week 2

I plan.

A month-long content calendar drafted for your restaurant. You approve. We adjust until it fits.

— Week 3

We shoot.

You send phone photos or I work with what you already have. I style, edit, and write captions.

— Week 4

It begins.

Posts go up on schedule. Stories run daily. We meet monthly to refine. Quietly, it compounds.
